<a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1J5iC8lZYphcikxR-fl6Vl9wfKjiuyewt/view?usp=sharing"> Mechatronics (MAE 3780) Final Report </a> This was the robot that won my team 3rd place during Cornell's annual Cube Craze Competition. The goal of the competition was to have this robot collect as many one inch cubes as possible within a minute. I worked on the mechanical design of it, such as the motors that controlled the arms and the steering system, while my teammates Joseph Kunken and Alan Munschy handled the coding and electrical aspects.
